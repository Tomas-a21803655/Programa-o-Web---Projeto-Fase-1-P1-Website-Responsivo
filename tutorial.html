Tutorial

Find out how to get the most of the Buddy Abroad app

  • Home

    In the Home screen you can see tours available near you and the top rated ones! Click the one that catches your attention, and find out more about it.

  • Search

    Here you can search for the perfect tour to enjoy during your stay.

  • Tour

    After choosing the desired tour, this screen allows you to find out more about it. In this screen you can discover the tour's details such as, it's duration, group size, languages available and many more aspects of the tour.

  • Bookings

    In the bookings tab you are able to perceive your bookings in a well organized agenda. You can quickly see the date and time of all booked tours and find out more about them by clicking the chosen tour.

  • Tour Creation

    In the Guide app you can create Tours in the create tab. Choose a title and a picture that best describe your tour and finish the creation process by filling out the rest of it's details.

  • Profile

    Create your profile in the profile tab and share with the rest of the users a few words about yourself, here you can also see your rating given by the tourist or guides depending if your a Guide or a Tourist.
